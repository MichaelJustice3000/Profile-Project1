About Me

I am The Doctor I am not from Earth, I am a Time Lord from the planet Gallifrey. I am the only survivor of the Time War against the Daleks.

I travel in the T.A.R.D.I.S. (Time and Relative Dimension in Space). The TARDIS is a Type 40 Time and Space Machine. The TARDIS is dimensionally transcendental, meaning it’s bigger on the inside than on the outside. The interior exists in a different, relative, dimension to the exterior.

The TARDIS is supposed to change its appearance on each trip to blend in with its surroundings however My Tardis has a broken chameleon circuit which prevents it from working properly and it has been in the form of a Police Box ever since.

I have a Sonic Screwdriver that uses sonic waves to dismantle or fix equipment. It can also bypass locks unless they have a ‘deadlock seal’.
